Who we are

We are a group of dreamers that came together in order to solve the growing polution problem. Nobody likes a dirty beach, and dirty beaches also affect the wildlife that live there. In order to tackle this, we decided to Design a robot that was able to take trash from the sand, no matter how big or small it may be. We took inspiration from the Ocean Cleanup interceptorâ„¢, which collects trash from rivers around the world. However, beaches still need to be cleaned up, so we decided to make our own robot, the Sandrunner. With it, we hope to slowly, but surely, clean up the world's beaches. With 80% of trash on beaches being plastic, however, we needed a way to make sure that the plastic we collected would not end up on beaches again. So, we decided to partner with Waste to Wear, a company that converts used plastics into clothes for people. We intend to donate a piece of clothing to homeless shelters for each piece bought, helping people get back on their feet.
