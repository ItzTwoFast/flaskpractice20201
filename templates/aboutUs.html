Who we are

We are a group of dreamers that came together in order to solve the growing polution problem. Most of the trash in the ocean is plastic, and they end up in the ocean because people litter on beaches, and when the tise rises, trash is swept away into the ocean. Plastic isnt biodegradable, meaning it will never fully go away. This means that when it breaks down, it will create microplastics that leach into the earth and ocean. These microplastics get introduced to the food chain when small microbacteria and animals, such as plankton and shrimp, injest it. This can cause bigger animals to not get the nutrients they need when they eat these smaller animals, weaking havoc in the food chain.
